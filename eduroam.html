How to configure your eduroam securely

  • Don't setup your eduroam on Android manually, because it is not secure!
  • Use the automatic setup of the TUM Campus App (recommended) or use a profile from Eduroam CAT.
  • If configured wrongly, third parties are able to see your password in plaintext.
